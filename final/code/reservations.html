Reservation Options

Our basic package is a nice 800 square foot room with a queen size bed, tub and shower, and all the appliances you would need to prepare for your temple visit (ironing board, steamer, shoe shine, etc.). Breakfast is included.

The Terrestrial Package is a wonderful addition to the Telestial Package. With all of our basic commodities and comforts, this one also comes with access to a pool and sauna, the on-site gym, and lunch is added on to your stay.

Our Celestial Package is for those looking to make their trip to the temple more of a vacation. Along with everything else offered in packages below, this package comes with a corner suite, a king sized bed, a 70-inch OLED television, and dinner and dessert included.

Our highrest package comes with all the bells and whistles. Included in this package are local excursions, a hotel-provided chauffeur for the entire duration of your stay available from 6 AM to 11 PM, unlimited drinks at our virgin bar, and a plus one per guest to be used at the gym, pool, sauna, or any other hotel facility.
