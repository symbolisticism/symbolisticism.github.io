The Gilbert, Arizona temple was the fourth temple to be built in Arizona. It was announced in the April 2008 General Conference of the Church. Ground was broken in 2010, officially starting the construction process. On March 2, 2014, the temple was dedicated for the work of the Lord. The dedication was preceded by a cultural celebration the night before, where thousands of youth gathered together to celebrate the house of God.
