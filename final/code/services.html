Services for Guests

  • 1. Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes
  • 2. A playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons
  • 3. Long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries
  • 4. Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • 5. A wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving
  • 6. A family search center that supports family search and ordinance work

Receptions

Spacious Ballrooms

At Holy Places Inn, we have three ballrooms that are regularly rented out for special events, including wedding receptions. We are happy to accomodate all weddings accomodated by a sealing in the closest temple.

Setup Included

Our head of receptions will meet with you to show you the spaces provided. In a quick 20-30 minute meeting, either in person or by video call, we can determine how things are to be set up and how we can prepare for your big day.

Luncheon/Dinner Included

Included in the reception package is your choice of one of three cuisines to be provided at your luncheon/dinner. We serve various Italian, French, and English dishes. Take the stress off yourself and allow us to prepare a delicious meal so you can enjoy every moment.

Photography Extra

For an extra fee, we are happy to provide a professional and reputable photographer for your special occasion. This includes photos and video footage of the night.

Full-Time Temple Missionaries

We love our full-time temple patrons. We know they are the backbone of temple operations. We have made special accomodations for full-time temple missionaries.

Extended Rentals

For full-time temple missionaries, we offer small-house sized suites that we charge on a monthly basis. These suites come with kitchenettes and 2 guest rooms so that you can have your family come visit. No contract is required.
