The beginning of great things happening in the community.

We are working on bringing great things to our city by creating a chamber of commerce. We believe that by creating a chamber of commerce, we can encourage engagement at events, foster growth in the youth, and create a sense of belonging. This will in turn boost the business and economy of Hogsmeade and make for a well-rounded and tight-knit community.
