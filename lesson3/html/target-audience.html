Our Target Audience

Our target audience will be comprised mostly of business owners and community members in the Hogsmeade area. Our website will be targeted towards business owners who are looking to help their business thrive, leaders in the community who want to see changes, and community members who want to be involved.

Barba Whipthrash

Our first persona is Barba Whipthrash, who is a bookstore owner in Hogsmeade. She has a husband and two kids at home. She is active in her kid's school parent organization and in the organization S.P.E.W. She thinks the upcoming generation is veering away from books, and she wants to help them see the magic in reading again. She joined the HCOC in order to help her business thrive and to encourage people to dive deeper into ancient literature and complex spellbooks.

Maximo Strale

Our second persona is Maximo Strale, a community member who practices the art of meditation for the purpose of increasing spell potency. He is not married because he has devoted his life to the study of meditation. Connecting to the people around him is one of his priorities, so he decided to join the HCOC in order to feel a greater connection to his community. He wants to find more friends and mentors to learn from, and he wants to contribute to other people's learning and development.

Five Scenarios

See Upcoming Events

People in the Hogsmeade area will visit the website to see what events are going on in town. Families with young kids know what it's like to need to get out of the house for a while. The website will have a discover page that will always display the latest news on events and activities in town.

Tour Your Town

People who have recently moved in and people who've been here a long time will both have an interest in getting to know our town better. On the home page, there will be weather updates, important news, business spotlights, and more. People looking to know Hogsmeade better will come to the page to find new things they want to try, or they will find information that will help familiarize themselves with the day-to-day in Hogsmeade.

Join the Board of Directors

Business owners who want to encourage the local economy and community members who want to contribute to the overall wellness of the town will visit the website to to find out how they can join the board of directors. Getting involved is the best way to make a difference, so we are going to include a section on the website for a form where people can express interest in joining and also send in an application. The more people who join, the better.

Get Contact information

This website will contain a working directory - a living document keeping track of all the businesses in Hogsmeade. People will visit this website to find contact information for different businesses to get business hours, prices, phone numbers, and other information. Having the contact information for businesses in one central location will help many business gain traction. For example, traffic in different parts of the city is probably varied, but in the directory, all the businesses have equal real estate. This will connect people with businesses, and it will help to spread the wealth around the town.

Add a Business to the HCOC

Membership in the HCOC is open to all. Some visitors will use the site to add their own business to the organization, so they can have the benefits of membership for their businesses and for their personal lives. We will offer different levels of membership for different price levels that include different benefits depending which level visitors buy.
