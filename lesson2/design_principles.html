White Space and Clean Design

Honeywell

honeywell.com

The Honeywell mobile web page is a good example of white space and clean design. You can see that the pictures and text have enough negative space for each element to exist in its own space. The web page has good, clean alignment throughout, which contributes to a harmonious layout that is attractive to the eye. The pictures are of professional quality.

Fitt's Law

Nike

nike.com

Nike's mobile web page is a good example of Fitt's Law. You could reasonably say that Nike's main objective is to get you to buy a pair of their shoes. Nike has made it extremely convenient and quick to get to the shopping page and the shopping cart. Any customer on the home page is one button push away from the shopping cart and two button pushes from the shopping pages.

Repitition

Disney

disney.com

Disney's mobile web page uses repetition to drive the user towards a Disney+ subscription. If you scroll down the page, you see that there's a lot of content that points towards things that are only available on Disney+. By repetitively mentioning their subscription service and showing what is exclusively available to subscribers, they are able to better convince people to subscribe.
